# Responsible Social Media Checklist for College Students

## Do's
* **Audit Privacy Settings:** Regularly check who can see your posts and personal info.
* **Curate Professionalism:** Use platforms like LinkedIn to showcase projects and skills.
* **Pause Before Posting:** Take a moment to reflect on the potential impact of a post.
* **Positive Networking:** Use social media to connect with mentors and industry peers.
* **Set Boundaries:** Monitor your screen time to ensure it doesn't impact your studies.

## Don'ts
* **Post Inappropriate Content:** Avoid sharing photos of illegal or policy-violating acts.
* **Share Sensitive Data:** Never post your student ID, dorm address, or real-time location.
* **Engage in Cyberbullying:** Refrain from harassment or spreading unverified rumors.
* **Rely on Social Media for News:** Verify information before sharing to avoid misinformation.
* **Compare Your "Behind-the-Scenes":** Don't let others' "highlight reels" affect your self-esteem.
